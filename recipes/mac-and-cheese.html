Mac and Cheese

Description

Easy to make homemade mac and cheese recipe that everyone will love!

Best served with sloppy joes, fried chicken, or a salad.

Ingredients

  • Macaroni
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Cheese
  • Seasonings
  • Bread Crumbs

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles
  2. Make the cheese sauce
  3. Make the topping
  4. Bake the mac and cheese
